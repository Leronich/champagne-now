Our Commitment to Transparency

Champagne.now is an independent editorial site. We earn revenue through affiliate partnerships — meaning we may receive a commission when you click certain links and make a purchase or booking. This disclosure explains exactly how this works.

How Affiliate Links Work

When you click an affiliate link on Champagne.now and complete a qualifying action (such as booking a hotel, purchasing a wine, or reserving a tour), we earn a small commission from the partner at no additional cost to you. The price you pay is identical whether you arrive via our link or directly.

Editorial Independence

Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial content. We recommend hotels, tours, producers, and bottles because we believe they are genuinely worth visiting, booking, or drinking — not because of commission rates. No partner pays to be featured or recommended on this Site. If a link doesn't feel earned by the prose, we don't include it.

Identifying Affiliate Links

Affiliate links on this Site are marked with rel="noopener sponsored" in the HTML. In editorial text, links to bookings, purchases, or partner experiences are contextually described — we do not use "click here" language.
