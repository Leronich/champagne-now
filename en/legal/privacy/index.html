Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Who We Are

Champagne.now is an independent cultural website dedicated to Champagne wine, the Champagne region of France, and its art de vivre. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our Site.

2. Information We Collect

Usage data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about how visitors use the Site — pages visited, time spent, referring URLs, browser type, and approximate location (country/city level). This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally.

Local storage: We store two items in your browser's local storage: your age verification response, and your cookie preference. No personal data is stored.

Contact: If you email us at contact@champagne.now, we will receive and store your email address and the contents of your message solely to respond to your enquiry.

3. How We Use Data

Analytics data is used solely to understand how the Site is used and to improve content. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except as described in this Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 for anonymous traffic analysis. Google may collect data subject to Google's own Privacy Policy. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.

Affiliate partners: When you click an affiliate link, the partner site may set cookies to track the referral. See our Affiliate Disclosure for details of our partners.

Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts, which may result in requests to Google's servers. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Cookies

See our Cookie Policy for full details of the cookies used on this Site.

6. Your Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. As we collect minimal personal data, requests can be directed to contact@champagne.now.

7. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for 14 months as per Google Analytics default settings. Email correspondence is retained for as long as necessary to fulfil your enquiry.

8. Children

This Site is not intended for persons under the legal drinking age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact
