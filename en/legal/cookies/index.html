Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Champagne.now uses a minimal approach to cookies — we collect only what is necessary to operate the Site and understand how it is used.

How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features. For Google Analytics specifically, you can opt out using Google's browser add-on.

Third-Party Cookies

When you click affiliate links on our Site, third-party partners may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies. Please refer to the privacy policies of the relevant partners for details.
